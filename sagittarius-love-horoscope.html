General Overview

  • The 9th sign in the Zodiac
  • Symbol: The Archer or Centaur
  • Date: November 22nd – December 21st
  • Element: Fire
  • Quality: Mutable
  • Ruling Planet: Jupiter
  • Color: Violet, Purple, Red, Pink
  • Day: Thursday
  • Body part: Hips, Thighs
  • Strengths: generous, idealistic, inspiring, encouraging, enthusiastic
  • Weaknesses: argumentative, flaky, impatient, reckless, tactless

Sagittarius are inquisitive and energetic wanderers always looking for change. They are adventurers and explorers who have an enthusiasm for life. One of their favorite things in life is roaming around the world to experience different cultures. Sagittarius values their freedom and can’t be tied down. They have an innate sense of curiosity and they are the type of people who leap without looking. They are courageous and always up for an adventure. Since they are well traveled, Sagittarius often brings new insights to different situations. Their nomadic lifestyle keeps them busy and they seem to have a million different things going on at once.

Sagittarius is the sign that encourages us to expand our horizons and continually learn. They are philosophical and value higher education. As a fire sign like Aries and Leo, Sagittarius sparks universal wisdom and action. Under this sign, we seek new challenges and possibilities. Ruled by Jupiter, Sagittarius desires the best things in life. This isn’t always a good thing and Sagittarius are hedonistic and self-indulgent. Despite this negative part of their personality, Sagittarius is one of the wisest and funniest signs.

In Love

When it comes to romance, Sagittarius are shameless flirts. However, they’re the type of people who need to sow their wild oats and they hardly settle down. They value their freedom and can’t stay in one place for too long. Later on in life when they’re done with their adventures, Sagittarius are happy to seriously look for a partner. This person needs to be intellectual, sensitive, and willing to explore the world with Sagittarius. Sagittarius are funny and playful, so they need someone who is equally outgoing. When they are in love, Sagittarius are loyal, devoted, and faithful. They will always see the good in people and need. To make a relationship work with Sagittarius, things need to have variety and stay exciting. There’s nothing Sagittarius hates more than stale complacency.

How to Attract Sagittarius Men

Sagittarius are fun loving and nomadic guys. His mind is always wandering and he’s thinking up the next big project or adventure in his life. To win him over, you need to do his best to capture his attention; appeal to his intellectual side and he’s sure to be yours. He’s a smart guy who loves having meaningful conversations. You have to show Sagittarius that you appreciate his mind and respect his opinions. Sagittarius values his freedom more than anything, so don’t be a clingy lady. To make things work with Sagittarius show him how smart you are and he’ll be sure to listen to your every word.

How to Attract Sagittarius Women

Sagittarius women are wild, independent, and friendly. These women can’t be tamed and they are determined to live their life to the fullest. She loves the outdoors and she is looking for a man who can keep up with her on her adventures. Sagittarius loves long conversations, but be sure to keep things light and interesting; she absolutely hates anything boring. She is a fierce woman who will always speak her mind. If you want to be with her, you can’t try to hold Sagittarius back. If you want to have everlasting love with a Sagittarius woman, keep things in your relationship straightforward and simple. In love, she hates anything complicated and will easily walk away from the relationship.

Best Match: Gemini

Gemini and Sagittarius will have a fun and easygoing relationship. They will communicate well with each other and easily have many great conversations. These two signs have a mutual respect for one another and they will ultimately stay faithful. Even if they break up, Sagittarius and Gemini will remain good friends. They have a deep understanding of one another and they will work hard to get through their challenges together. Since they’re both fond of adventures, Sagittarius and Gemini will enjoy sharing their experiences and learning new things together. Though both signs are typically not emotional, when they get together all of their feelings come pouring out. Sagittarius and Gemini make one of the best matches and together they will have an innocent and pure love.

Worst Match: Taurus

Taurus’ practical and serious nature will clash with Sagittarius’ energetic and adventurous ways. Sagittarius thinks that Taurus is too safe and not spontaneous enough for their liking. Unlike Sagittarius, Taurus hates change. Their natural characters are too far apart and these two will never understand each other. Sagittarius is just too fiery and Taurus is too down to earth. In this relationship, the fighting will never end; Sagittarius and Taurus will never learn to trust each other. This pair sees will always see the worst in each other. Neither one will is willing to compromise to get along.
