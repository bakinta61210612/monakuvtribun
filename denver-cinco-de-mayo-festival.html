Description

The largest cultural event in Colorado is happening again at Denver's Civic Center Park the weekend of May 6th & 7th from 10am to 8 pm. The Cinco de Mayo Festival is back and ready to celebrate. Bring your family and friends and join us for a community parade, the best food and vendors, special events and contests, 3 stages of live performances, exhibits and much more! Admission is free.
