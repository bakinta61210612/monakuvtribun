Stu West is one of the most popular and richest Bassist who was born on December 24, 1964 in Lincolnshire, England, United Kingdom. Stu West (born 24 December 1964, Lincolnshire, England) was the bassist for the English punk band the Damned from 2004 to September 2017. He joined in 2004, when the past bassist Patricia Morrison left the band due to pregnancy. She had a daughter, Emily, by her long-time partner and Damned vocalist, Dave Vanian. Morrison never rejoined the band. It was announced on 11 September 2017 that West is no longer with the band, having been replaced by Paul Gray.
