EXCLUSIVE: Premiere of &quot;All In&quot; EP from October Rose

 Posted on August 14, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  276 words  |  Tandra Barner

Here at CelebMix, we love being able to give you exclusive access to new music and today, we are delighted to bring you the exclusive premiere of the debut EP “All In” from October Rose.

October Rose is a duo made up of Leanne Weiss and Derek Allan, two artists who were both pursuing careers as solo artists when they connected via social media. After relocating to New Jersey, Derek reached out to Leanne about writing country music together, and after various songwriting sessions and the recording of a cover video, the two decided to form October Rose in October 2016.

Fast forward two years and the duo have already been making waves in the country music scene, a genre which is massively growing at the moment. The duo has opened for seasoned country artists including Sara Evans, Drew Baldridge and Russell Dickerson, They have also released their debut single “Heartbreak Song” which is continuing to gain traction and radio adds.

This Friday, the duo are set to release their debut EP All In and you can listen to it before anyone else, exclusively here at CelebMix. The EP stays true to Leanne and Derek’s country roots, taking country-pop elements and incorporating a rockier edge. As well as their single “Heartbreak Song” the EP features five other songs – “All In”, “Bonfire”, “Heart Can’t Lie”, “Drink It Up” and Closer.
