20 of the hottest women in the world

 Posted on September 2, 2024  |  8 minutes  |  1613 words  |  Patria Henriques

When it comes to the question of the hottest women in the world, there is a very large sample size to choose from. From actresses, models and musicians there are many beautiful women around today, maybe more than ever before. Read on to find out who we chose as our top 20 overall right now.

Narrowing down to 20 was not easy, but we hope we did this list justice. Our picks for 20 sexiest women currently are as follows below.

Hottest celebrities 2019

1. Beyoncé

Known as Queen B, she has conquered everything from music, business and now in the movie industry following the release of Disney’s The Lion King. The movie is currently tearing up box office records all over the world, and her role in this cannot be overlooked. Everything she touches turns to gold, as has been the case for the past few years since releasing her Lemonade album. For this reason, she tops the list as our choice for the most beautiful woman in the world.

2. Charlize Theron

Celebrated actress Charlize Theron who is South African is one whose beauty is undeniable. She was even declared at one point to be the sexiest woman alive by Esquire Magazine in 2008. She has won several awards including the Academy Award for Best Actress in the movie Monster. She has been in recent successes such as Mad Max Fury Road, Furious 8 and Ocean’s 8, showing she is still very popular.

3. Rihanna

This Jamaican artist is known most for her music, but has also been making waves recently for her Fenty Beauty line of cosmetic products. She is one of the sexiest women in the world and was declared the sexiest one by Esquire Magazine in 2011. She has also been featured in movies such as Battleship, Home and Ocean’s 8 alongside Charlize Theron. She has also won a Grammy Award, the highest honour in music. A lot of people you ask might say she is the most beautiful girl in the world.

4. Serena Williams

This renowned athlete has been dominating tennis for several years now. She is revered by both fans and those who are not familiar with the sport. She has several high-profile endorsements to her name as well as stunning magazine covers. It is not hard to see why she is considered by many, including us, the sexiest woman in athletics.

5. Ashanti

This musician and actress has long had the reputation of being one of the most gorgeous women ever. Even without much in the way of new music on her part, she is still very much in the public eye. Most recently she went viral with her pictures from the Carnival celebrations in Trinidad and Tobago.

Most attractive women 2019

Now known as Meghan, Duchess of Sussex, this former actress is now a member of the British royal family. Her royal wedding to Prince Harry was a surprising event that caught everyone off-guard, but she has managed her new life very well. She was famous (before joining the royal family) for her performance as Rachel on the series known as Suits.

7. Chrissy Teigen

This beloved personality is one of the hottest models ever. She is known all over the world for her warm personality as well as her quick wit. She is also a bestselling author who has written two cookbooks.

8. Kaley Cuoco

Known for her roles as the girl next door in shows such as 8 Simple Rules for Dating My Teenage Daughter and The Big Bang Theory, Kaley Cuoco is one of the most gorgeous women in the world. She has won awards such as People’s Choice Awards and Teen Choice Awards. We are waiting eagerly to see what her next move will be after concluding the very successful show The Big Bang Theory.

9. MJ Rodriguez

This incredibly talented actress and singer is best known for her role in the popular series Pose. She is also a model and has appeared on a few magazine covers. She won the Clive Barnes Award for her role in an Off-Broadway musical. She is one of the most talented sexy women making a name for themselves now.

10. Margot Robbie

She is an Australian actress who has appeared in some successful movies, such as Suicide Squad and The Wolf of Wall Street. Her acting has earned her nominations for awards such as BAFTA and the Academy Awards. She was also named as one of the most influential people in the world by Time Magazine and one of the top 30 under 30 by Forbes.

11. Gal Gadot

This Israeli actress is known best for her performance as Wonder Woman in movies such as Justice League and Wonder Woman. But most do not know that she is also a model and has also studied law. Time Magazine included her in their list of most influential people in the world and she is also one of the highest paid actresses. She is definitely in the running for the title of the sexiest woman in the world.

12. Keira Knightley

This English actress is best known for her role as Elizabeth Swann in the Pirates of the Caribbean franchise. She also had amazing performances in movies, such as The Imitation game as well as Pride and Prejudice. She is also involved in charity work, such as Amnesty International and causes against domestic abuse. She has been honored by Harper’s Bazaar Women of the Year as well as ELLE Women of the Year.

13. Lupita Nyong’o

This Kenyan-Mexican actress has had an incredible career over the last few years. Since appearing in the movie 12 Years a Slave, she has captured out hearts. She has gone on to win an Academy Award and star in other significantly successful movies such as Star Wars The Last Jedi, Black Panther and Us. She studied at Hampshire College as well as the prestigious Yale. People Magazine named her the most beautiful woman in the world and we agree.

14. Jennifer Lopez

Most of you would not believe it, but she is an astonishing 50 years old. She has aged very gracefully, and it is one of the many reasons why she is on this list. She has had a very colorful career in music and acting. She is a legend in the music industry and has sold millions of albums. She has made popular songs such as Jenny from the Block and If You Had My Love. Her movies include Gigli, Maid in Manhattan and Anaconda. She has appeared on Time Magazine’s list of the most influential people in the world more than a few times. Among the hundreds of awards she has won, there are two different Guinness World Records for First Number 1 Remix Album on the Billboard 200 and Highest Viewed Female Music Video of All Time. She is one of the most beautiful women in the world.

15. Naomi Campbell

This world-famous supermodel is one of the hottest women in the world. Since the 1980s, she has had a very long and successful career that does not seem to be ending any time soon. Apart from that, she is involved with charity work, such as the Nelson Mandela Children’s Fund.

This emerging actress is one of the beautiful ladies making it big in Hollywood right now. Since starting her career in the music industry, she has transitioned to movies. She is in one of the current box office hits, Spider-Man Far From Home. She is also involved in charity work as part of the Convoy's Women's Empowerment Initiative.

17. Priyanka Chopra

She is an Indian actress, model and singer. She is most known for her performance in the series Quantico. She has won numerous awards for her acing work. Before acting, she was interested in pursuing a career in aeronautical engineering. She is also involved in charitable organizations such as The Priyanka Chopra Foundation for Health and Education, which she founded, of course, as well as UNICEF.
