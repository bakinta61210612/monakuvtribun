Valkyrae real name?

Valkyrae is the online alias of Rachell Hofstetter, who was born on January 8, 1992, in Moses Lake, Washington.

She is of Filipino and German descent and has three brothers and one younger sister.

She credits her mother for introducing her to video games at a young age.

How old is Valkyrae?

As of 2023, Valkyrae is 31 years old.

She started streaming on Twitch in 2015, when she was 23 years old.

She graduated from community college with an associate degree in arts and science and worked at GameStop before pursuing streaming as a full-time career.

Valkyrae height

Valkyrae is 5 feet 3 inches (160 cm) tall.

She often jokes about her height on her streams and social media posts.

She once tweeted that she was “the shortest person in the room” when she met other streamers like Pokimane, Sykkuno, and Disguised Toast.

Valkyrae net worth

According to some sources, Valkyrae has an estimated net worth of $4 million.

She earns money from her YouTube channel, which has over 3.9 million subscribers and over 439 million views, as well as from sponsorships, donations, and merchandise sales.

She also owns a stake in 100 Thieves, a gaming organization founded by former Call of Duty player Matthew “Nadeshot” Haag.

Conclusion

Valkyrae is a successful and influential streamer who has made a name for herself in the gaming industry.

She is known for her skills, personality, and collaborations with other popular creators.

Valkyrae has won several awards, such as the Game Award for “Content Creator of the Year” in 2020 and the Streamy Award for best live streamer in 2021.

She was also named “Gaming Creator of the Year” by Adweek in 2021 and was included on Forbes’ 30 Under 30 list in 2022.

Valkyrae is an inspiration to many aspiring streamers, especially women, who want to pursue their passion for gaming.
