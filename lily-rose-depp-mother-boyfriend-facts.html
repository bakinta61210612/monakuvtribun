Lily-Rose Depp (Johnny Depps Daughter); Mother, Movies, Boyfriend, Age, Brother

Following the footstep of her father, Lily-Rose Depp has demonstrated her acting skill, appearing on numerous movies. Meanwhile, her remarkable work earned her several awards and nominations.

American-French by nationality, Lily-Rose Depp is recognized as the daughter of the award-winning actor Johnny Depp. She is also an actress, who started her career from a cameo role in the 2014 film Tusk.

Lily-Rose Depp Family; Who Is Her Mother?

Lily-Rose Melody Depp was born on 27 May 1999 in Neuilly-sur-Seine, France, a city west of Paris. She is one of the two children of Johnny Depp and the French singer, actress, and model Vanessa Paradis. Her parents began dating in 1998 and ended their relationship after 14 years in 2012.

Lily-Rose Depp and her brother Jack arrives in Paris to celebrate their mother’s 45th birthday in December 2017 (Picture: Daily Mail)

Lily-Rose a younger brother, John “Jack” Christopher Depp III, born on 9 April 2002. Unlike her, Jack enjoys private life and does not prefer to appear in public. In June 2018, the news emerged regarding his health situation. His mother Vanessa was not able to attend the premiere of new film A Knife in the Heart because of his serious health problems.

Lily-Rose is close to her parents and often divides her time Los Angeles and Paris to meet them. She and her brother attended some prestigious private schools in Los Angeles. However, she dropped out of high school in 2016 to focus on her acting career.

Who Is Her Mother?

Vanessa Paradis became a star at an early age of 14 after the success of her single “Joe le taxi“. Vanessa has appeared in numerous movies such as White Wedding (1989), Girl on the Bridge (1999), Heartbreaker (2010), Yoga Hosers (2016) and so on.

Lily-Rose Depp looks like her mother Vanessa Paradis (Picture: Instagram)

Apart from Johnny Depp, Paradis has been in a relationship with numerous names. Her dating list includes Florent Pagny (1988 to 1991), Lenny Kravitz (1992 to 1997), Stanislas Merhar (1997 to 1998), and Benjamin Biolay (2012 to 2015). In June 2018, she married her boyfriend Samuel Benchetrit in the small French town of Saint-Simeon.

Career; Movies, TV Shows

Depp started her acting career at the age of 15 from the 2014 comedy body horror film Tusk. In 2016, she was cast as Colleen Collette in the 2016 comedy horror film Yoga Hosers. In the same years, she starred in the movies The Dancer and Planetarium. She earned the Most Promising Actress nominations twice at 42nd César Awards and 22nd Lumières Awards for her work in The Dancer.

In 2018, Lily-Rose appeared in the movie Savage and A Faithful Man. She landed the role of Catherine of Valois in the 2019 historical drama film The King. Her upcoming movies includes Dreamland, Voyagers and Silent Night.

Dating Status; Who Is Her Boyfriend?

The actress is now rumored to be dating her The King co-star Timothée Chalamet. The duo started dating after meeting on the set of The King in 2018. Later, they were spotted walking arm-in-arm in Paris in December 2018.

Lily-Rose Depp and Timothée Chalamet walking together during a date night in New York City in October 2018 (Picture: E! News)

Since then, the pair have been appearing in numerous events together. In September 2019, they appeared in the red carpet for the premiere of The King at the Venice Film Festival in Italy. Depp and Chalamet, who were on-screen couple on the movie are yet to confirm their relationship.

Some Interesting Facts About Lily-Rose Depp

  • Lily-Rose bagged the 4th Los Angeles Film Awards for Best Actress for her role in My Last Lullaby in 2019.
  • Both the mother Vanessa and daughter Lily-Rose co-starred in the 2016 horror comedy Yoga Hosers.
  • Her godfather is an American singer, songwriter, record producer, actor, visual artist, and former music journalist Marilyn Manson.
  • In 2015, she got featured on the music video of Irish rapper Rejjie Snow’s song “All Around the World.”
