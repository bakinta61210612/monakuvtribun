Diddy Son King Combs: The Son of a Legend

King Combs was born Christian Casey Combs on April 1, 1998, in California.

He is the son of rap legend Diddy and model Kimberly Porter, who passed away in 2018.

He has six siblings: Quincy Brown, Justin Combs, Chance Combs, D’Lila Star Combs, Jessie James Combs, and Love 1.

King grew up surrounded by music and celebrities.

He celebrated his Sweet 16 in style by having Kendrick Lamar, French Montana, and Meek Mill perform.

He also attended his high school’s senior prom as a sophomore.

King was inspired by his father and other rap icons like The Notorious B.I.G., Jay-Z, Nas, and Drake.

He started making his own rap music and uploading it on YouTube under the WorldStarHipHop channel.

He also signed with his father’s label Bad Boy Records and formed a collective called CYN with his friends.

King Combs: The Rising Star

King Combs made his debut in 2016 with the single “Party” featuring French Montana.

He followed it up with more singles like “Type Different”, “Love You Better”, and “How You Want It?”.

He also collaborated with artists like Chris Brown, Ty Dolla $ign, Jeremih, and Smooky MarGielaa.

In 2018, King released his first mixtape called 90’s Baby, which paid homage to the golden era of hip-hop.

He sampled classic songs like “Crush On You”, “All Eyez On Me”, and “Shook Ones”.

He also showed off his lyrical skills and versatility on tracks like “Bad Boy Back” and “Eyez On C”.

In 2019, King dropped his second mixtape called Cyncerely C3, which featured guest appearances from City Girls, Tee Grizzley, 03 Greedo, and more.

He also showcased his growth as an artist and a storyteller on songs like “Heaven Sent”, “Good To Ya”, and “Surf”.

In 2020, King released his third mixtape called 90’s Baby 2: C3 Edition, which continued his tribute to the 90’s rap sound.

He also experimented with different genres like R&B, dancehall, and trap on tracks like “Legacy”, “Feeling Savage”, and “On It”.

In 2021, King dropped his fourth mixtape called C3: The Album, which marked his transition from a mixtape artist to an album artist.

He also delivered his most personal and mature project to date, addressing topics like love, loss, family, and fame on songs like “Cartis”, “Still Here”, and “Diana”.

In 2022, King released his fifth mixtape called C3: The Deluxe Edition, which added eight new songs to his previous album.

He also collaborated with Kodak Black on the hit single “Can’t Stop Won’t Stop”, which earned him a nomination for Best Collaboration at the BET Awards.

King Combs: The Future

King Combs has proven himself to be more than just Diddy’s son.

He has established himself as a talented rapper with a unique style and a loyal fan base.

He has also shown his versatility as an artist by exploring different sounds and genres.

King Combs is a rising star in the rap scene who has a lot to offer.

He is the son of a legend, but he is also a legend in the making.
