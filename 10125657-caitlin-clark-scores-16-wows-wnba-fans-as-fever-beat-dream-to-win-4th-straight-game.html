Caitlin Clark Scores 16, Wows WNBA Fans as Fever Beat Dream to Win 4th Straight Game | News, Scores,

 Posted on August 27, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  442 words  |  Patria Henriques
Adam Hagy/NBAE via Getty Images

The Indiana Fever beat the Atlanta Dream 91-79 on Friday behind a strong performance from Caitlin Clark to secure their fourth consecutive win.

The rookie had 16 points, seven assists and four rebounds to help guide Indiana to the win. The solid game from Clark followed up an 18-point, 12-rebound performance on Wednesday and a 23-point outing on Sunday.

Kelsey Mitchell and Nalyssa Smith also had impactful performances with 18 points and 21 points respectively.

After another Fever win, fans praised Clark and the team for turning things around after a rocky start.

The Fever seem to be hitting their stride after a rough start to the year. Indiana began the season 3-10 before the recent win streak has brought it to 7-10 and back in the playoff mix. With the win, the Fever remain the No. 8 seed, half a game ahead of the No. 9 seed Chicago Sky.

If Indiana can continue its momentum, it could reach the playoffs for the first time since 2016.

But the Fever have a tough slate ahead of them, facing the Chicago Sky, Seattle Storm, Phoenix Mercury and Las Vegas Aces over the next four games. That stretch will be a good test for the young Indiana team.
