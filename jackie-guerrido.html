Jackie Guerrido Biography

Jackie Guerrido is one of the most popular and richest Meteorologist who was born on September 24, 1972 in San Juan, Puerto Rico. Former wife of reggaeton singer Don Omar who has worked for Spanish-language TV news as a meteorologist.

She auditioned for the title role in the biopic Selena, but the part went to Jennifer Lopez.

In 2004, Guerrido was named the “Good Will Ambassador” of the Puerto Rican Day Parade celebrated in New York City. She is the mother of two teenagers, Thomas and Adieny. On April 19, 2008, Jackie married reggaeton singer Don Omar in Puerto Rico. In 2010, Guerrido participated in Mira Quien Baila!, a reality show, which aired on Univision in the United States and is the American/Spanish-Speaking version of Dancing with the Stars. The show’s finale was on November 21, 2010 and Guerrido came in 2nd place, by popular vote, to Vadhir Derbez. Guerrido was awarded $10,000 dollars (US) which she donated to the “Casa Protegida Julia de Burgos”, a domestic violence shelter.

In 2011 she was divorced from her husband, Don Omar.

She was married to Don Omar from 2006 to 2013. She has two sons named Tomás Ramírez and Adieny Núñez.
