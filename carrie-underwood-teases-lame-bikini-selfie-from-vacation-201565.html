Carrie Underwood Teases &quot;Lame&quot; Bikini Selfie From Vacation

 Posted on August 24, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  165 words  |  Patria Henriques

Sunning and stunning! Carrie Underwood teased her 2 million Instagram followers with a rare bikini-selfie on Tuesday, May 5 — but only from the shoulders up. 

In the pic, the "Cowboy Casanova" singer, 32, lounges on a yellow and white striped towel, her blonde hair tousled behind her and a pair of oversized, movie-star-glam sunglasses covering her eyes. The straps of her halter-style bathing suit are visible at the bottom of the frame, but the rest of her (no doubt awesome) bikini body is left to the imagination.
