Missi Pyle: A Closer Look at the Actresss Age, Height, Career, Relationships and Wealth

Missi Pyle is an American actress and singer.

She was born on November 16, 1972 in Houston, Texas.

Pyle began her acting career in the early 1990s, appearing in guest roles on television shows.

She has appeared on among other TV shows, Mad About You and Friends.

Her breakthrough role came in 1999, when she starred in the comedy film Galaxy Quest.

Pyle has since appeared in a number of other films, including Bringing Down the House.

Others are Dodgeball: A True Underdog Story and Charlie and the Chocolate Factory.

Missi Pyle age

She was born on November 16, 1972, making her 50 years old as of May 2023.

Missi Pyle height

She is 5 feet 11¾ inches tall, which is approximately 1.82 meters, and weighs 57 Kgs.

Missi Pyle net worth

Pyle has an estimated net worth of $3 million.

Missi Pyle career

Pyle has featured as a guest on many television shows, including Heroes and Boston Legal.

She has also featured on Mad About You, Frasier, 2 Broke Girls and The Sarah Silverman Program.

Pyle started her career in the film As Good as It Gets with a small role along with Jack Nicholson and Helen Hunt.

After her stunning role in Galaxy Quest, she got to perform some supporting roles in Josie and the Pussycats.

Pyle also landed a role in Bringing Down the House, Home Alone 4, Big Fish, Exposed and Soul Plane among others.

The talented actress is also known to be a singer.

In 2008, she released an album along with Shawnee Smith titled It’s OK to Be Happy.

Shawnee met Missi while filming Traveling in Packs, an ABC comedy pilot.

Missi Pyle relationships

Pyle married Antonio Sacre, an author, in 2000. However, the couple became separated in 2005.

After three years of separation, on 12 September 2008, Pyle married Casey Anderson.

Casey is a TV presenter known for hosting America the Wild and Expedition Wild with Casey Anderson, the Nat Geo WILD series.

Also, he is the managerial producer of both America the Wild and Expedition Wild with Casey Anderson series.

The wedding took place in Montana and was country-western themed.

Comedian Steve Agee and bandmate Shawnee Smith were invited as wedding guests.

Brutus, Casey’s pet bear, served as his best man at the wedding.

Pyle and Anderson also showed off their appearance in Grizzly Dogs, an episode of Dog Whisperer in October 2010, in which they rehabilitated their dog with the help of Cesar Millan.

Pyle’s relationship with Casey also didn’t work and in 2013, the couple confirmed their breakup.
