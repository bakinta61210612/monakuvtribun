Novak Djokovic is a brilliant player & also a great human being.

Novak’s objective is not just to win but also to aid children all around the globe. By founding the Novak Djokovic Foundation, he aims to give chances to children who cannot develop their full potential due to a lack of access to pre-primary education and good nutrition. Novak established the Novak Djokovic Foundation in 2007 in collaboration with the World Bank and UNICEF to assist poor Serbian children with the rights and opportunities they need to have a bright future and become contributing citizens worldwide.
