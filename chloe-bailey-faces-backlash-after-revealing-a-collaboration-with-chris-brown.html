Chloe Bailey Faces Backlash After Revealing A Collaboration With Chris Brown

 Posted on August 13, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  464 words  |  Kelle Repass

Twitter fans recently chastised American singer Chloe Bailey for partnering with Chris Brown on her forthcoming track, How Does It Feel? Several questioned Bailey’s choice to partner with someone who had previously been accused of violence towards women. People express their worry and displeasure over Chloe Bailey working with Chris Brown as the social media platforms continue to accumulate tweets and posts regarding the relationship. @jennytheleo posted:

“Chloe Bailey writing a song with Chris Brown in 2023; she’s frightened of success.”

After announcing her How Does It Feel? collaboration with Chris Brown, Chloe Bailey was criticized.

After the virality of Chloe Bailey’s February 16, 2023, the Twitter post announcing her collaboration with Chris Brown, some netizens rushed to social media sites to vent their displeasure. Some Twitter users, emphasizing Brown’s record as an abuser, remarked that the new collaboration was “disgusting” and requested Chloe to “remove” the post and the song from the album.

Nonetheless, numerous Twitter users hopped on the bandwagon to support Bailey, citing Chris’s talent as an artist. They reported that Bailey was “barely” receiving support from the internet community, but when Brown is mentioned, she is chastised.

Chris Brown lost many commercial contracts as a result of several complaints.

Chris Brown’s assault claims extend back to 2009 when he was dating Rihanna. At the time, the Umbrella singer was outspoken about Brown hitting her, which resulted in several injuries. He later pled guilty and admitted to hitting Rihanna at the time. He lost many business contracts as a result of the event and received a lot of criticism.

Shortly after, Brown’s other ex-girlfriend, model Karrueche Tran, obtained a restraining order against him. She accused him of following her, sending her derogatory texts, and attempting to physically hurt her. Brown received many charges after an incident with Frank Ocean, in addition to assaulting his ex-girlfriends and being engaged in domestic violence cases. Brown allegedly threatened to “shoot him,” according to the latter. In addition, he was engaged in a hit-and-run case in Los Angeles in 2013 but avoided prison time because the charges were withdrawn.

Chris Brown has previously been in treatment for untreated bipolar illness and post-traumatic stress disorder. Another lady filed a civil action against Brown in January 2022, claiming he of r*ping her aboard a boat in Florida. According to the complaint, the event occurred in December 2020.

But, when he re-entered the music industry, celebrities who have been seen in a single frame with him since then have experienced huge outrage. Similarly, former Fifth Harmony member Normani was chastised for collaborating with Chris Brown. How Does It Feel, Chloe Bailey’s next song starring Chris Brown, will be released on February 24, 2023. More tracks from her album, In Pieces, will be available a few weeks later.
