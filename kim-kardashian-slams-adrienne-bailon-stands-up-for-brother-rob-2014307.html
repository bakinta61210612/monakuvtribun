Kim Kardashian Slams Adrienne Bailon, Stands up for Brother Rob

 Posted on September 2, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  232 words  |  Patria Henriques

Celebrity feud alert! Kim Kardashian had brother Rob Kardashian's back on Wednesday, July 30, when she blasted Adrienne Bailon via Twitter for dissing her brother in Latina magazine.

As Us Weekly reported, the former Disney Channel star gave a no-holds-barred interview to the outlet about her relationship with Rob, revealing she believed appearing on Keeping Up With the Kardashians had harmed her career.

She also admitted she struggled to understand why people sided with the reality TV star, saying Rob cheated on her and was "disloyal."

"Funny how she says being with a Kardashian hurt her career yet the only reason she has this article is bc she is talking about a Kardashian," blasted Kim in the early hours of the morning.
