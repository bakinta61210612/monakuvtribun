The Shrink Next Door Season 2 Plot: What Is It About?

From the show’s Synopsis, it is pretty clear that actual events inspire this series. It is based on the bizarre relationship between a psychiatrist Dr. Isaac ‘Ike’ Herschkopf (Paul Rudd), and a long-term patient Martin ‘Marty’ Markowitz, which Will Ferrell plays. 

The doctor and patient get used to each other, and over time the charming doctor Ike finds a way of inserting himself in Marty’s life to the extent of moving in with him and persuading him (the patient Marty) to name him the president of the family business. 

This series, therefore, explores how a normal doctor-patient relationship develops into something exploitative. It turns out to be manipulative with lots of power grabs and dysfunction. 

With a dash of comedy, it is pretty clear why dark comedy lovers cannot wait for its premiere. Even as we patiently wait, let’s find out who is in the cast. 

The Shrink Next Door Season 2 Cast: Who Will Be In?

Who is starring in The Shrink Next Door? Paul Rudd (Ghostbusters), known for the comedy series Friends and Clueless, stars as the psychiatrist, Dr. Isaaac ‘Ike’ Herschkopf, who develops a bizarre relationship with Will Ferrell, known for Step Brothers and Zoolander. Will acts as Martin ‘Marty’ Markowitz. 

The series will also have Kathryn Hahn known for Bad Mom’s and We’re The Millers, acting as Phyllis, Mary’s sister. Casey Wilson (Atypical), known for Bride Wars and Gone Girl, poses as Dr. Herschkopf’s wife, Bonnie. 

With such a rich cast, it is safe to assume that this series will be worth every viewer’s time. We can only wait for what is ahead.
