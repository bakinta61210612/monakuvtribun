Early Life and Family

A popular Youtuber – Jack Doherty, was born on October 8, 2003, and now he is 20 years old. He is the son of Mark Doherty and Anna Doherty. Michael Doherty is his elder brother.

Jack spent his childhood in Long Island, New York, and graduated from North Shore High School.

His parents wanted to make him an athlete, but he chose to be a YouTuber instead. However, Jack’s parents are happy for him to pursue a career that he loves.

Career

On July 15, 2016, Jack embarked on his YouTube journey. His first video to go online was titled “Marker Flip Twice in a Row,” which now has 407K views.

Initially, he had 100 to 200 subscribers; later, his viewers and subscribers grew, and now there are 3.43 Million Subscribers on his channel. 

The most viewed video of Jack is titled “I Flipped all of These,” which has 28M views and was published five years back. New uploaded videos are also popular, and his channel has remarkable viewers.

Likewise, Floor is lava challenge at Walmart, Singing “DESPACITO” on the Walmart Intercom, Flips for a kiss at the mall, Kicked out of a Trampoline Park are some of his highest viewed videos.

Jack’s creative content flow that meets the demand of his subscribers is what leads him to gain such extreme views.
