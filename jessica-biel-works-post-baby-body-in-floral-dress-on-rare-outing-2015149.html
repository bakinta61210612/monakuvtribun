Jessica Biel Works Post-Baby Body in Floral Dress on Rare Outing

 Posted on September 9, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  161 words  |  Tandra Barner

Working it! On a rare outing, new mom Jessica Biel showed off her svelte post-baby body in a feminine, floral dress at Amazon Video's Tumble Leaf Family Fun Day in L.A. on Sunday, Sept. 13.

Looking fresh-faced, Biel, 33, paired her tan and green 2nd Skin Co. dress with natural makeup and her hair down in tousled waves. An olive-green belt highlighted her tiny waist, and white Manolo Blahnik sandals and Lorraine Schwartz baubles completed the ensemble.
